Getting Started

GestureRemote lets you control your Apple TV using hand gestures detected by your iPhone's camera. Point your iPhone at yourself, make sure your Apple TV is on the same Wi-Fi network, and tap your Apple TV's name in the app to connect.

Supported Gestures

  • Thumbs Up – Select / OK
  • Thumbs Down – Back
  • Open Palm → – Swipe Right
  • Open Palm ← – Swipe Left
  • Open Palm ↑ – Swipe Up
  • Open Palm ↓ – Swipe Down
  • Palm → Fist – Menu

Tips

  • Use in a well-lit room for best recognition accuracy.
  • Hold gestures steady for about half a second until the on-screen icon appears.
  • Make sure the app has camera and local network permission in iOS Settings.
  • If your Apple TV doesn't appear, tap Rescan or use Manual IP to connect by IP address.
